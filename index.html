About The Confluence

Department of Applied Geophysics

Established during the International Geophysical Year in 1957, the department is a premier center for geophysical teaching and research in India. We offer M.Sc. Tech, Integrated M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs.

Admitting 55 M.Sc. Tech, 21 Integrated M.Tech, 25 M.Tech and 10 Doctoral students annually.

Geophysical Society & Chapters

GeoConfluence is organized annually by the Geophysical Society volunteered by researchers and students. We actively collaborate with international student chapters to execute academic and industrial interactive events.
